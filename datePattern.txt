Regular  Expression (in JS flavor), which matches dates in YYYY/MM/DD HH:MM(:SS) format
NOTE! Using regex101.com remember delete 'Newline' symbol after copy-paste solution to Regular Expression window
My solution is:
^(([0]{3}[1-9])|([1-2]([\d]{3})))(\/{1})(([0][1-9])|([1][0-2]))(\/{1})(([0][1-9])|([1-2][0-2])|([3][0-1]))(\ {1,})(((([0-1][0-9])|([2][0-4]))(\:{1})([0-5][0-9]))|((([0-1][0-9])|([2][0-4]))(\:{1})([0-5][0-9])(\:{1})([0-5][0-9])))$
